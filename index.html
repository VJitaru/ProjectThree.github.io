Professional organizing

Professional Organizing emerged as an industry in 1984 within Los Angeles.[1] A professional organizer assists individuals and businesses to improve their organizing systems and process. The purpose hereof is to improve the quality of life, achieve more productivity and greater efficiency. Professional organizers help facilitate the efficient redirection of dynamic long-tail paradigms to leverage more useful cross-applications that ensure properly co-sustainable, synergistic futures for their clients' spaces and processes.
